# Askeal
 
## AI-powered cybersecurity assistant built on community expertise
 
Askeal helps cybersecurity and IT teams access reliable, explainable, and actionable cybersecurity knowledge through AI grounded in trusted expert sources.
 
It combines AI reasoning with validated expertise contributed by cybersecurity vendors, researchers, independent experts, and research organizations.
 
---
 
# What is Askeal?
 
Askeal is a cybersecurity assistant designed to help users investigate threats, understand vulnerabilities, analyze security findings, and make faster operational decisions.
 
Unlike general-purpose AI tools, Askeal answers are built from specialized cybersecurity knowledge sources with transparent attribution and traceability.
 
The platform is designed for:
- Security teams
- IT teams & IT help desk managers
- SOC analysts
- System & network administrators
- DevOps & engineering teams
- Cybersecurity experts & vendors
 
---
 
# How Askeal works
 
Askeal combines AI reasoning with trusted cybersecurity expertise contributed by the cybersecurity community.
 
Contributors can be:
- Security vendors
- Researchers
- Independent experts
- Research labs
- Security teams and organizations
 
Their expertise can be shared through documents, articles, APIs, or MCP integrations, and may include:
- Threat intelligence
- Vulnerability research
- Security reports
- Playbooks
- Technical analysis
- High-level cybersecurity knowledge
 
Askeal analyzes, validates, and correlates these sources to generate reliable and actionable AI answers with transparent attribution.
 
Every answer references the expertise and contributors that helped produce it.
